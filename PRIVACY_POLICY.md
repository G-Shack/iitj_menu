# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** December 22, 2025

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how **IITJ Menu** ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses your information. This app is developed by **Om Tathed** for the IIT Jodhpur community.

## Information Collection and Use

**We do not collect any personal data directly.**

The IITJ Menu app is designed to display the mess menu for students and faculty. It fetches menu data from a remote configuration but does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information (such as name, email, phone number, or location).

## Advertising

This app displays advertisements provided by **Google AdMob**. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads. This may include:

*   Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
*   IP address
*   General location (country/region)
*   App usage data

You can opt out of personalized advertising:
*   **iOS:** Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
*   **Android:** Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services to function:

*   **Google AdMob:** Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device and usage data for ad targeting.
*   **Firebase Remote Config:** Used to fetch the latest menu data.
*   **Firebase Analytics:** Used to collect anonymous usage data (e.g., screen views, button clicks) to help us improve the app. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users.
*   **Firebase Crashlytics:** Used to report app crashes so we can fix bugs.

These services may collect information used to identify your device for diagnostic purposes, but this is not linked to your personal identity by us.

## Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data directly, there is no risk of your personal data being breached from our servers. However, third-party services (AdMob, Firebase) have their own data handling practices as outlined in their respective privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is intended for college students and faculty.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
