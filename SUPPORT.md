## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Menu & Content

**Q: The menu is not loading.**  
A: Please check your internet connection. The app caches the menu for offline use, but it needs an internet connection to fetch the latest updates. Try pulling down to refresh.

**Q: The menu information is incorrect.**  
A: The menu is updated based on the official mess schedule. If there is a discrepancy, please email us with the correct details.

**Q: Why does the app scroll to a specific meal when I open it?**  
A: The app automatically scrolls to the current or upcoming meal to save you time. If all meals for the day are over, it shows the full day's menu from the top.

### App Features

**Q: How do I switch to Dark Mode?**  
A: The app follows your device's system theme automatically. Change your phone's display settings to Light/Dark mode.

**Q: How do I filter for Veg/Non-Veg?**  
A: Use the toggle switch in the header to filter the menu items based on your dietary preference.

**Q: How do I view a different day's menu?**  
A: Use the day selector (Mon, Tue, Wed...) at the top of the screen to switch between days.

**Q: How do I check the app version?**  
A: Tap "Contact Me" at the bottom of the menu screen. The version number is displayed in the popup.

### Advertisements

**Q: Why are there ads in the app?**  
A: Ads help support the development and maintenance of this free app. We've placed them at the bottom to minimize disruption to your experience.

**Q: Can I remove ads?**  
A: Currently, there is no ad-free version. The app is free and ads help keep it that way.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app crashes on startup.**  
A: Try the following:
1. Ensure you have the latest version from the App Store
2. Restart your device
3. Reinstall the app
4. If the issue persists, email us with your device model and iOS version

**Q: Ads are not loading.**  
A: This is normal if you've just installed the app. Ad networks may take some time to start serving ads. This doesn't affect app functionality.
